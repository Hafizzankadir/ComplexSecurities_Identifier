Using the ETF Complex Securities Identifier

A quick walkthrough for staff

1Configure your AI settings

Click Analysis Settings in the header to enter your Gemini API key, choose a model, customise the analysis prompt, and adjust the confidence score rubric. Your API key is stored locally in your browser only.

2Load your ETF list

Upload a spreadsheet (.xlsx, .csv, .xls) containing ETF tickers and, ideally, a column with each fund's Prospectus Link. After upload, confirm which column holds the ticker symbols and which holds the prospectus link. Alternatively, expand "Or Enter Tickers Manually" to paste tickers directly (one per line) — note that manual entries will not have a prospectus link unless added afterward.

3Run the automated analysis

Click Start Automated Analysis. The tool will, for each ETF: identify its country of origin classify the fund as Complex only if derivatives, leverage, synthetic replication, or structured notes form the fund's main/primary strategy (minor or incidental derivative use for cash management, hedging, or liquidity purposes is treated as Non-Complex), write a short commentary explaining that distinction, and assign a confidence score that reflects how clearly the prospectus supports that dominant-vs-minor judgement. Progress, elapsed time, and completion time are shown in the run-status card. You can pause and resume at any time, or re-run a single row using the refresh icon in its row.

4Explore the dashboard

Use the search box, classification filter, country filter, and confidence range filter above the table to narrow down results. The summary cards and country breakdown panel update live to reflect whatever filters are active.

5Export your results

Export Excel downloads a spreadsheet of whatever rows currently match your dashboard filters, preserving your original columns and adding Classification, Commentary, Confidence Score, and Confidence Basis columns. PDF Report opens a printable audit report that also reflects your active dashboard filters, and notes which filters were applied along with analysis timing.

Tips
  • Use Get Sample CSV to download a template with the expected columns.
  • The Activity Console at the bottom-left logs every action and error in real time.
  • Use Reset All to clear the session and start fresh; this cannot be undone.
